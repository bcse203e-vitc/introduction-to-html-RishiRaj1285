Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

J.K. Rowling

"Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" (also published as "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone") is the first novel in the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling. It introduces the orphaned Harry Potter, who learns on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard and has been accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The story follows Harry as he navigates the magical world, makes new friends (Ron and Hermione), discovers his past, and confronts the evil Lord Voldemort.
